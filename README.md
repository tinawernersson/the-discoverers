## This is The Discoverers Preschool

The Discoverers Preschool want to educate small children into good and independent citizens by taking care of their own interests and abilities. The preschool teachers can then make the education more idividual-based so every child can participate on their own terms.

The site
-is informative
-have a preschool spirit with playful fonts and colors
- have a simple navigation design to make it easy for users to navigate on the page
