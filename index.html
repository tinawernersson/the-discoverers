About Us

We are a preschool where all children in the ages of one to five are welcome. The children's curiosity is the key to education and development, and that is where our foundation lies. We let the children explore their surroundings with the support of adults.

The Discoverers Preschool looks after the children's own interests and works to strengthen and develop independent individuals. Based on the children's needs and maturity, we adapt the teaching and the environment so that it suits everyone and where everyone should have the opportunity to develop according to their own abilities.
